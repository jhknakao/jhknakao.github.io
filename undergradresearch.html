Interested in Doing Research With Me?

On this page you will find the qualifications and expectations for participating in undergraduate research with me. Depending on your needs, projects will generally take two or more semesters to complete. That being said, please expect to spend at least one full school year working with me. If a position is currently open, then feel free to apply! Whether your goal is graduate school, industry, the national laboratories, or something else, hopefully we can find an exciting project for you! If you have any questions or concerns regarding undergraduate research, please do not hesitate to send me an email.


Undergraduate Research Positions
  • There are currently no open positions. Applications will open in 2024.




Qualifications and Expectations

Required Qualifications
  • Minimum 3.5 cumulative GPA.
  • Successful completion (grade B+ or higher) of at least one of the following courses: MATH27, MATH28 (preferred over MATH27), MATH43, MATH44.
Preferred Qualifications
  • Moderate fluency in either MATLAB, Julia, or Python. Other languages are acceptable, but we will mostly code in MATLAB and Julia.
  • Successful completion (grade B+ or higher) of any courses on the following topics: numerical analysis, partial differential equations, real analysis, fluid mechanics, electrodynamics, heat transfer
  • Fluency in LaTeX. I will teach you LaTeX if you do not already know it.
Expectations
  • Minimum 6 hours of work per week (on average) during the school year.
  • Weekly meetings.<\li>
  • Occasional written reports and (short) presentations on your progress and results.
  • Perseverance! Coding-heavy research can be quite frustrating at times and will require a lot of perseverance. However, I completely understand this and am very patient as long as I see you working hard.
